Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How is the safety and security of local stays ensured?

We verify all local hosts through a rigorous process and provide reviews and ratings from previous guests. Additionally, our platform offers 24/7 customer support to handle any issues that may arise.

What is the refund policy in case of cancellations?

Our platform offers a flexible refund policy depending on the cancellation time frame. You can view the detailed policy in the booking section or contact our support team for more information.

How are prices for stays, food, and rentals set?

Prices are set by the local hosts based on market rates and the services offered. The platform ensures transparency by displaying all costs upfront, with no hidden charges.

What is the process to register as a service provider?

To register, simply sign up on our platform, complete the verification process, and create your listing. Our team will guide you through each step to ensure a smooth registration process.

Are there any partnerships with local tourism boards or organizations?

Yes, we collaborate with local tourism boards and organizations to promote sustainable tourism and enhance the travel experience for both tourists and locals.
